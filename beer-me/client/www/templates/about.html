About the App

Beer-Me! is an app designed to help the discerning beer connoisseur find rare and interesting beers.

Small-production craft brews and microbrews are often only available at a few restaurants or bars. They are unpredictable to source and find. This app helps you discover what unusual beers are on tap in your neighborhood. Simply press the "Beer-Me!" button to see a list of the rarest beers nearby.

Try a new beer today! It might be your next favorite.
